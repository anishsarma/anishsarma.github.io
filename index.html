Anish A. Sarma

I am a medical student and engineer. I completed a Ph.D. at Caltech and I am working towards an M.D. at Vanderbilt.

Outside of clinical medicine, I work on mathematical optimization and control for experiments, interventions, and foundational understanding in immune, regenerative, and cancer biology. I am also interested in building out the core tools of this work, where possible, for applications in medicine, ecology, neuroscience, and synthetic biology.
