Anish Sarma

I'm a control theorist working on mathematical and computational tools for tech interfaces with medicine and climate. I'm writing my Ph.D. dissertation at Caltech, advised by John Doyle and co-advised by Lea Goentoro. Previously, I developed neural interfaces with the BrainGate clinical trial.
